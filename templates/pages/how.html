How it works

octoSwitch is a prototype audio switch intended for DIY multi room setups!

It is implemented as a standalone web app that runs on a Raspberry Pi Zero W which is connected to the electronic circuit handling the audio switching. In it's current form, the switch handles 2 audio outputs, which can play at the same time or play separately.

NOTE: The volume controls currently do not work, and are there for visual testing.
